VISA

If you’re entering on an international bus, your bags may well be
searched. Tourist cards for citizens of most countries
cost US$10. Valid for up to 90 days, it grants repeated access to
Honduras, Guatemala and Nicaragua.

WEATHER

May–Aug The surf gets heavy; bring a spare board just in case.
Early August brings a week-long celebration of El Salvador's
patron saint.

CURRENCY

El Salvador's official currency is the US dollar. ATMs are plentiful,
and credit cards, particularly visa cards, widely accepted.
Restaurants - Tip 10%, Taxis - It is not customary to tip taxi
drivers, though rounding up the amount is appreciated
